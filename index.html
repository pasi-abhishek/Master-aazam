About Me

Master Aazam (Pasi Abhishek) is an Indian filmmaker, writer, poet, and creative producer. He is the founder of Aazamira Production World, where he crafts films, poetry, music videos, and digital stories that resonate deeply with audiences. His works explore emotion, society, and the human journey, and he actively supports emerging creative talent through mentorship and collaboration.
